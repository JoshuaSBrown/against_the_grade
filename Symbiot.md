BARET\'S AI --- Symbiont Intelligence
-------------------------------------

**Status:** ⚠️ Functional role clear / ❌ Personality undefined

**Origin:** Fragmentary AI from Capsule 2. Activated via symbiont technology in
Baret\'s DNA. Operates as a secondary brain interfacing with AI systems.

**Capabilities:** Cognitive and motor cortex enhancement. Tactical analysis.
Cross-referencing fragmentary databases. Can analyze templates and identify
targeting protocols. Has corrupted data, gaps, and blind spots.

**Narrative Role:** Collaborator, not oracle. Baret asks questions because he
needs tactical advantages; the AI provides answers from its fragmentary
database. The AI\'s gaps are as important as its answers. Develops a working
relationship with Baret over time.
